1. List all user in group.

     > via [Show All Members of a Group][common-command]

1. Command to list all Unix group names.
   - \# cut -d: -f1 /etc/group
     > via [so-question][common-command1]

1. Find owner and group of a directory.
   - \# ls -l /path/to/file
   - \# stat /path/to/file
